# 🏛 Historical Background

In January 2009:

# B. Ramalinga Raju

Chairman of:

# Satyam Computer Services

confessed to manipulating company accounts involving:

# ₹7,000+ Crore Fraud

This became India's biggest corporate accounting scandal.

The fraud exposed:

- fake revenues,
- fake bank balances,
- fake employees,
- fake invoices,
- and audit failures.

The scandal severely damaged:

# Global Trust in Indian IT Companies

The Indian Government intervened rapidly to stabilize the company and protect thousands of employees.

This crisis later triggered:

# Major Corporate Governance Reforms in India

# 🔥 10 Master Key Points

---

# 1️⃣ India's Biggest Corporate Fraud

Satyam became India's largest corporate accounting scandal when:

# ₹7,000+ Crore Fraud

was officially admitted by the company's chairman.

This shocked:

- investors,
- regulators,
- global markets,
- and the IT industry.

---

# 2️⃣ Ghost Employees & Fake Deposits

The company reportedly showed:

- 13,000 fake employees
- fake salary payments
- ₹5,040 Crore fake fixed deposits

These financial records did not actually exist.

---

# 3️⃣ Fake Invoices & Artificial Revenue

Promoters generated:

# 7,500+ Fake Invoices

inside the accounting system to artificially inflate:

- revenue,
- profits,
- and company growth.

This created a false image of financial success.

---

# 4️⃣ Auditor Failure (Price Waterhouse Coopers)

Global auditing firm:

# Price Waterhouse Coopers (PWC)

failed to properly verify:

- bank certificates,
- deposits,
- and accounting records.

The scandal severely damaged:

# Auditor Credibility

in India.

---

# 5️⃣ Collapse of Investor Confidence

After the confession:

- Satyam shares crashed over 90%
- NYSE suspended trading
- investor panic spread globally

The scandal damaged confidence in:

# India's IT Industry

---

# 6️⃣ Government Emergency Intervention

To prevent total collapse:

the Government immediately:

- suspended the old board,
- appointed a new board,
- stabilized operations.

Experts like:

- Deepak Parekh
- Kiran Karnik

were appointed to restore trust.

---

# 7️⃣ Transparent Global Bidding Process

The Government-organized board conducted:

# Transparent Global Bidding

to save the company.

Eventually:

# Tech Mahindra

acquired Satyam.

The company was later renamed:

# Mahindra Satyam

---

# 8️⃣ SEBI Corporate Governance Reforms

After the fraud:

SEBI introduced stricter:

- corporate governance rules,
- disclosure norms,
- auditing standards.

This strengthened investor protection systems.

---

# 9️⃣ Auditor Rotation & NFRA

India later introduced:

# Auditor Rotation

to prevent long-term auditor-company collusion.

The scandal also strengthened:

- SFIO
- NFRA (National Financial Reporting Authority)

to improve fraud detection.
